Sentiment Analyses of Twitter Data
Comparison of machine learning sentiment
analysis of “jobs” tweets against traditional survey data.
Index of Consumer Sentiment (ICS) Traditional survey method used to evaluate the populations’ attitudes toward the economy.
Machine Learning Algorithms: TextBlob & Vadar Two machine learning algorithms were used to assign sentiment scores between -1 and 1 to jobs related tweets with the intention of determining if the algorithms can serve as a full or partial replacement to costly traditional survey methods.
UNCERTAINTY The machine learning algorithms of TextBlob and Vadar provided differing sentiment scores for each tweet. This visualization explores the differences between those two values as a source of uncertainty within the data. The range between the values is demonstrated through a connecting line that expands and contracts within the space between the points. At a more detailed zoom level, the points move back and forth within the space between their values to demonstrate the range in which the tweet’s sentiment falls based on the algorithm selected
